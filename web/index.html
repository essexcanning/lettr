Press and hold to speak. Lettr will listen, ask clarifying questions, and start hunting.

"I'm Stephen. I want a 1-bed in zones 1–2, under £2,400, by mid-June, good light, quiet street, pet considered."

What happens after you talk:

  1. Lettr stores your taste vector in MongoDB Atlas Vector Search.
  2. Searches 30 London listings against it, picks the top match.
  3. Emails the letting agent on your behalf.
  4. If the agent ghosts, Lettr calls them with a polite voicemail.
  5. Reports back via voice, and tracks every interaction in the reputation graph →
Built at the MongoDB Agentic Evolution Hackathon, London · 2 May 2026 · Stack: MongoDB Atlas Vector Search + LangGraph (with MongoDB Checkpointer) + LiveKit + ElevenLabs + Fireworks AI · Repo: github.com/essexcanning/lettr
